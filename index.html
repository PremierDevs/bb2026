The Concept— What this is & why it works

Each week we feature a different Paris-area burger, score it with our office panel, and let our followers vote. After six weeks of weekly battles, the top three burgers face off in a championship round — and the winner gets crowned Paris's Best Burger. We're not running a contest. We're throwing a community-wide spotlight on local eateries, one burger at a time.

04

Weekly Content— What we post for every burger

The Tasting

30–60 second visit to the restaurant. Agent walks in, orders, takes the first bite on camera. Tag the restaurant. Drop on Tuesday.

The Hero Shot

Single beautiful burger photo + caption with the scorecard breakdown. Clean, magazine-style. Drops Wednesday morning.

The Vote

Behind-the-scenes from the visit, plus the "rate this burger" sticker poll. Live for 48 hours. Reshare top responses.

The Standings

Friday standings update on stories — "where did this week's burger land?" Drives anticipation for the next round.

The championship Round

Top 3 burgers from the regular season go head-to-head. 100% public vote, 72-hour window, results announced live on Instagram. Winner gets a custom "Paris's Best Burger 2026" window decal — year-round branding for them, year-round visibility for us.

05

Restaurant Outreach— How we get them on board

The Approach

Reach out 2–3 weeks before launch. In-person visit beats email. We pay for every burger — it keeps the scoring honest and our audience trusts the results. If a restaurant offers to comp the meal, we politely insist on paying.

Most restaurants will say yes immediately. A few may want to host us at a slow time — that's fine, work with them.

The Ask

One visit. 3–4 agents. We order, tip well, film a quick reel, tag them everywhere. No cost to them, no commitment. Just good local press.

"Hey — we're Premier Realty Group, and we're running a burger battle this fall featuring local Paris spots. We'd love to feature yours on [date]. We'll bring 3 or 4 agents in, order off the menu, tip well, film a quick reel, and tag y'all on Instagram and Facebook. No cost to you. We just love what you're doing and want to spotlight it. Sound good?"
